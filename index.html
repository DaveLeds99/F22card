F-22 Raptor (Lockheed Martin)

5th generation Air Superiority Fighter (considered 4th Gen by USAF)

Max Speed: Mach 2 +

Flight roof: 65,000 ft

Range: Aprox 1,500 nmi

Unit cost: 339 mill USD per plane (estimated)

The F-22 was designed to be highly difficult to detect and track by radar, with radio waves reflected, scattered, or diffracted away from the emitter source towards specific sectors, or absorbed and attenuated.

First Flight: September 7th, 1997
